Account Tier Features

The Account tier represents business entities that operate within a tenant marketplace:

Supply Side (Agencies)
  • Manage multiple talents under one account
  • Bulk operations for portfolio updates
  • Team management with role-based access
  • Commission tracking and reporting
  • Agency profile and branding
Demand Side (Clients)
  • Advanced talent search and filtering
  • Project management and collaboration
  • Saved searches and talent collections
  • Direct booking and contract management
  • Company verification and branding
